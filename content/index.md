## Key dates

[[calendar|The Semester 1 calendar]] runs September 2026 to January 2027 — every
attainment and end-of-term assessment at its date, alongside term boundaries, holidays,
and school-wide anchors.

First teaching day is **Tuesday 1 September 2026**.

## How grading works

Four categories, fixed across every course: **Attainment** (50%), **Course Skills** (20%),
**Learning Behaviour** (10%), and the **End of Term** exam (20%). The full scheme, with the
notice you're owed before any assessment, is in
[[shared/bnds-assessment-framework|the assessment framework]].

Term dates and holidays are on [[calendar|the calendar]], alongside every assessment.
