# School Academic Calendar

**Beijing National Day School (BNDS), A-Level Programme — Semester 1, 2026–27.** This page is the authority on dates. If a course plan disagrees with this page, this page wins and the plan needs fixing (note the conflict, don't silently rewrite).

**About the source file:** despite its filename, `school-semester1-calendar-2026-27.xlsx` is not a bare school calendar — it is a course-planning template (its masthead reads "Art & Design 9479", an artifact) whose fully populated *school layer* carries the BNDS Autumn Calendar 2026-27. That school layer is what this page extracts. The course layers in that copy (units, assessments, status) are empty. All five course schedule workbooks were generated from the same design system and embed this same school layer.

> [!warning] Corrected 2026-07-19 — this replaces a fragmented reading
> The source calendar showed Christmas break as **three separate days** (12-24, 12-25, 12-28) with 12-29 to 12-31 apparently live, and New Year as a TBC 01-01→01-03. The human confirmed it is **one continuous school-wide break from Thu 2026-12-24 to Sun 2027-01-03, returning Mon 2027-01-04.**
>
> Consequences: **Tue 12-29, Wed 12-30 and Thu 12-31 do not teach.** The 12-29 Seniors' Day and the 12-31 BNDS Carnival both fall inside the break and are moot for teaching purposes. **New Year's Day itself is 01-01**, but the break around it is wider than the calendar implied.
>
> This kills dated work in two courses — see the sweep note below.

## Cohort lens caveat

The school layer in the source file **assumes a G11 course**: it blocks the G11 Juniors Days and marks Seniors' Days as G12-only context. Courses with other cohorts must re-derive their own no-class days — as the file itself puts it, "CONFIRM IT, DO NOT INFER IT." (The Pre A Level workbook already corrected this: its shipped layer wrongly blocked Juniors Days for a G10 class.)
