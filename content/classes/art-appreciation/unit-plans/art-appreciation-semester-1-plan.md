# Art Appreciation Semester 1 Plan

> [!note] Your weekly schedule
> **Mon P4 (45') · Tue P6+7 (double, 90') · Wed P1 (45') · Fri P2 (45').** Four teaching days, five periods, one double, 225 minutes a week. **There is no Thursday session.**

> [[classes/art-appreciation/art-appreciation|Art Appreciation]] · Semester 1 · next: [[classes/art-appreciation/unit-plans/art-appreciation-semester-2-outline|S2 outline]]

**Mixed G11+G12, Autumn 2026-27.** 15 deliverable-based lessons across **65 teaching days / 82 periods**. A lesson is one deliverable, not one day: if a day is lost, the deliverable does not move — the beats compress and the cut beat is named. Dates follow the [[calendar|school calendar]].

Because this is a **mixed G11+G12 room, both cohorts' absence blocks affect the course** — G11 Juniors Days (11-04→06, 12-02→04) and G12 Seniors' Days (the 10-26 week, 11-26/27). Attendance matters: a lost Wednesday costs a whole unit stage.

## Standing protocols

Session spine 45': 5 land / 10 look / 20 work / 10 close (one student says what changed, one what is stuck — not optional). Critique: **describe → account → judge, never out of order**; the describe round is written before it is spoken. Assessment review within 5 working days: criteria + 10 minutes' silence to find your own gap, one strength + one move, written; the next lesson opens with the move. Homework ≤30 min/day; break homework 3–4 h per ten-day holiday.
