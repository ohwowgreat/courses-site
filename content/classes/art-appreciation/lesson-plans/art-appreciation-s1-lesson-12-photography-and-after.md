## Goal

A4 is the last thing you submit this semester. It clears the roughly 1 January late-work cutoff by two weeks, which means nothing in this stretch can be handed in late. The synthesis is the second constellation of the year; the Unit 1 boards go back up on day four so you can measure this one against your first.

## How it runs

- **Day 1 · Fri 11 Dec · `U4·L12`** — what the poor image gains: what circulation gives that quality takes.
- **Day 2 · Mon 14 Dec · `U4·L13`** — photography and the claim to truth: why we believe photographs, and whether we should. Sontag reading set.
- **Day 3 · Tue 15 Dec · `U4·L14`** — Sontag: photographing as an act on the world.
- **Day 4 · Wed 16 Dec · `U4·L15`** — avant-garde and kitsch: test Greenberg's line and find the place it stops holding. Unit 1 boards back on the wall.
- **Day 5 · Thu 17 Dec · `U4·L16`** — **A4 sits**: build the constellation and write the argument, 40 minutes.
- **Day 6 · Fri 18 Dec · `U4·L17`** — synthesis debrief: work returned with the criteria, and the one move that would have sharpened it.

## Assessment

- **A4** — Unit 4 synthesis constellation, 40 minutes. Marked on arguing across the whole unit, not a selection of favorites. The exact sitting date will be confirmed in class.
- Review is in-lesson on day six — the only in-lesson review this term, done while the work is still live.
