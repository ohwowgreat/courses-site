## Goal

The synthesis on day five, feedback on day six — and A4 is the last thing submitted this semester, clearing the ≈1 January late-work cutoff by a fortnight, "which is deliberate and which means nothing in this plan can be handed in late." The synthesis is the second constellation of the year "and it should be visibly better than the first": put the Unit 1 boards back up on day four.

## How it runs

- **Day 1 · Fri 11 Dec · `U4·L12`** — what the poor image gains: what circulation gives that quality takes. Foreign Culture Day risk: "if this falls, nothing graded goes."
- **Day 2 · Mon 14 Dec · `U4·L13`** — photography and the claim to truth: why we believe photographs, and whether we should. Sontag set.
- **Day 3 · Tue 15 Dec · `U4·L14`** — Sontag: photographing as an act on the world; the charge taken seriously.
- **Day 4 · Wed 16 Dec · `U4·L15`** — avant-garde and kitsch: test Greenberg's line, find the place it stops holding rather than agreeing with it. Unit 1 boards back on the wall.
- **Day 5 · Thu 17 Dec · `U4·L16`** — **A4 sits**: the constellation is built and the argument written, 40 minutes.
- **Day 6 · Fri 18 Dec · `U4·L17`** — synthesis debrief: returned silent with the criteria; "the one move that would have sharpened it." Assessment review protocol.

## Assessment

- **A4 sits Tue 2026-12-15** — Unit 4 synthesis constellation. Marked on arguing across the whole unit, "not a selection of favorites."
- Review is in-lesson on day six — "the only time that happens this term and it works because the work is still live. Protect it."
