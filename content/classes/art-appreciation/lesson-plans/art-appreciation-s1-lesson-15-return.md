## Goal

Three days after the exam window and after the grades deadline, so nothing here carries weight into the Semester 1 grade — and the students will know it. "That is not a reason to waste them. It is the only unhurried time in the year to talk to eleven people one at a time about what they actually made."

## How it runs

- **Day 1 · Tue 19 Jan** — work returned; one-to-one feedback while everyone else re-reads their own work against the criteria.
- **Day 2 · Wed 20 Jan** — one-to-one continued. Grades and comments due today.
- **Day 3 · Thu 21 Jan** — Semester 2 trailer: patronage, the market, and who decides what is worth money; write the goal. LB9 posted.

## Assessment

- **LB9** — Tue 2027-01-19, posted after the Wednesday grades deadline, "so it counts toward Semester 2, not this one. Say so, or it looks like a trick."
- The Semester 2 goal is the deliverable — "about a practice rather than about a grade."
