## Overview

These three days fall after the exam window and after the Semester 1 grades deadline, so nothing here counts toward your Semester 1 grade. They are time to talk about the work you actually made.

## How it runs

- **Day 1 · Tue 19 Jan** — work returned; one-to-one feedback while everyone else re-reads their own work against the criteria.
- **Day 2 · Wed 20 Jan** — one-to-one continued. Grades and comments due today.
- **Day 3 · Thu 21 Jan** — Semester 2 trailer: patronage, the market, and who decides what is worth money; write your goal. LB9 posted.
