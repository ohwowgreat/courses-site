## Goal

Three days, then Christmas. The content is "the end of the story the course has been telling since the caves": what makes something art when looking cannot tell you. "Duchamp and Warhol are the punchline and the students will have been waiting for them." Day three sets the break homework, and the break homework **is** the retrospective announcement — Wednesday 23 December is fifteen days before Thursday 7 January and the last session before the break, so the notice covers the whole holiday. "If day three goes, the retrospective has no notice."

## How it runs

- **Day 1 · Mon 21 Dec · `U4·L18`** — Danto: the artworld and the end of art, the argument taken straight.
- **Day 2 · Tue 22 Dec · `U4·L19`** — Warhol, Duchamp, and pluralism: follow the object out of the studio and into the institution — who decided, and where. LB8 posted. End-of-Semester Activity today: expect a short room.
- **Day 3 · Wed 23 Dec · `U4·L20`** — **RETROSPECTIVE ANNOUNCED** (Thu 7 Jan, in class), then the break homework: three works, with reasons, 3–4 hours, not more.

## Assessment

- **LB8** — Tue 2026-12-22, posted on the Activity day — "a posting does not need a full room, but do not put anything on Tuesday that has to land."
- **Retrospective announced** Wed 2026-12-23 — 15 days' notice covering the holiday; the brief doubles as the break homework.
