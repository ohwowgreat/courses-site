# 9607 Semester 1 Plan

[[classes/media-studies/media-studies|Media Studies]] · Semester 1 of 4 ([[classes/media-studies/unit-plans/9607-course-map|course map]])

**G11 (AS year), autumn 2026-27.** 16 lessons across **64 teaching days in 21 weeks**. Over the semester you master media language and textual analysis, produce and complete Component 1 by **Fri 2026-11-27**, and establish C2 Section A craft. Dates follow the [[calendar|school calendar]] and may shift when the school publishes the calendar for this period.

Classes are Mon/Tue/Fri singles plus a **Wednesday double**. Every in-class assessment sits in the Wednesday double.
