## What this unit does

The semester's knowledge is audited, drilled and sat. A4 feedback becomes targets; the coverage matrix crosses every case study against the four Key Concepts to find the gaps HW2 fills over the break; exam rehearsal makes the paper boring — format, timing, and the mark scheme's AO economy all rehearsed; then the full two-hour Component 2 sits in the school exam window and returns as question-level AO data and one-page Semester 2 target sheets.
