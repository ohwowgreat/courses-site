## Goal

On the Wednesday double before the break, you lock your C1 plan on the blog to a shootable standard — a plan someone else could shoot without asking questions. HW1 is set the same day because A1's 14-day notice window falls inside the holiday. The Friday return is a debrief (annotated, not graded) and your first sound and editing vocabulary, which your shoots will put into practice.

## How it runs

- **D1 (Mon, single):** Todorov — equilibrium, disruption, new equilibrium — tracked on one short narrative, then applied to your own C1 plan: where is the disturbance in your opening.
- **D2 (Tue, single):** Propp character functions, input and mapping (by what they do, not who they are); **LB1 posted** (blog punctuality, posts 1–5); ten minutes in the room to bring your blog up to date.
- **D3 (Wed, double): planning lock** — full peer review of every planning suite (storyboard/flat plan, shot list, schedule, roles) against the shootability checklist, describe–question–suggest; **HW1 set**, due Wed 10-14, exactly 14 days; post and date your final plan before leaving.
- **D4 (Fri 10-09, post-break):** sound and editing vocabulary taught against your own break footage where you have it; HW1 debriefed — annotated, not graded; **LB2 posted** (returned on time, glossary current).
