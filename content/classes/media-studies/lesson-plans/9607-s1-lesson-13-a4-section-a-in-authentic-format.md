## Goal

A4 is your Forecast Grade anchor. Over two days you trace one institution across ownership, distribution, audience and regulation. Wednesday is your first Section A sitting in the real format: thirty minutes of viewing and note-making before forty-five minutes of writing. This is a different skill from A1's single hour, and the briefing treats it that way. Friday adds the regulation context while the sitting is fresh.

## How it runs

- **D1–2 (Mon+Tue block):** integration, synergy and convergence defined and found in one institution's real output; on day 2, the same institution is traced end to end. You update your case-study documents live, and each day ends with a two-minute structured-notes drill.
- **D3 (Wed, double): A4 sits** — 5 min setup / 30 min viewing and notes / 45 min writing / 10 min collect. You get a briefing on what the viewing window is for, and the sitting runs exactly to the paper's clock. There is no debrief today: the format itself is the lesson.
- **D4 (Fri, single):** regulation context — who regulates the case-study institution and what that changes about its output; add dated entries to your document.
