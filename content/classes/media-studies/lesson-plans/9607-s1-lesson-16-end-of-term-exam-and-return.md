## Goal

The semester closes the way Semester 2 will open: with evidence. The full two-hour Component 2 paper is sat in the exam window — the only place a genuine two-hour sitting fits. Scripts come back Tuesday with question-level AO feedback, and the Wednesday double converts that data into one page per student: where the marks went, which AO is the gap, and what Semester 2 does about it. Grades and comments are due the same Wednesday; the target sheets are written to survive being read in February.

## How it runs

- **Exam (window Mon 01-11 → Mon 01-18):** full Component 2, 2 hours, exam venue, full conditions; slot set by the exam office; **book screening facilities for Section A**. Per the approved counting convention this is an assessment event inside the lesson, not a taught day.
- **D1 (Tue 01-19, single):** scripts returned with question-level AO breakdowns; assessment review protocol at full depth — self-assessment before marks, gaps named in writing.
- **D2 (Wed 01-20, double):** target sheets built from the AO data — gap, evidence, action, review date; **LB7 posted** (response to feedback, S2 goal setting); Semester 2 preview closes the year — coursebook units U6–U8, C2 Section B depth. **Grades and comments due today.**
