## What happens

You sit the full Component 2 paper (2 hours, exam venue, full conditions) in the window Mon 01-11 → Mon 01-18. Your exact slot within that window is set by the exam office. Scripts come back on Tuesday with question-level AO feedback. On the Wednesday double you turn that feedback into a one-page Semester 2 target sheet: where your marks went, which AO is the gap, and what Semester 2 does about it. Grades and comments are issued the same Wednesday.

## How it runs

- **Exam (window Mon 01-11 → Mon 01-18):** full Component 2, 2 hours, exam venue, full conditions.
- **D1 (Tue 01-19, single):** scripts returned with question-level AO breakdowns; assessment review — self-assessment before marks, gaps named in writing.
- **D2 (Wed 01-20, double):** target sheets built from the AO data — gap, evidence, action, review date; **LB7 posted** (response to feedback, S2 goal setting); Semester 2 preview — coursebook units U6–U8, C2 Section B depth. **Grades and comments issued today.**
