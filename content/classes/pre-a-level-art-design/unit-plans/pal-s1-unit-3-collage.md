## What this unit does

Spine: **"Combine · meaning."** Five Wednesday double sessions (W11–15) move from single images to combinations: juxtaposition and scale — "two images say a third thing" — then layering and text-with-image, then drafting toward one final collage made in class for A3. It is the semester's longest unit and also its most exposed: both TBC calendar events (field trip, project week) land inside it.
