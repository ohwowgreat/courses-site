# Unit 4: Graphic Design & the Poster

[[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/pre-a-level-art-design|Pre A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/unit-plans/pal-semester-1-plan|Semester 1 Plan]] · Unit 4 of 4 · ← [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/unit-plans/pal-s1-unit-3-collage|Unit 3: Collage]]

## What this unit does

Spine: **"Communicate · audience."** This unit runs across three Wednesday doubles (W16, W17, W19 — there is no class in W18 because of the winter break). You move from composition to communication: one session analyzing what a poster must do (message, audience, hierarchy) and testing thumbnails and type, then the final poster — handmade or digital — for A4, then documentation. The last session closes the semester with the End-of-Term portfolio **and the A5 sketchbook submission** (both handed in that day).

There is one teaching session before the assessment, so missing a Wednesday in this unit costs a large part of your preparation.

## Two routes through the same slot

This unit runs in one of two forms in the same three Wednesdays: **Poster** (the default, described below) or **Printmaking** (linocut — see the [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/pal-s1-u4-printmaking-variant|variant plan]]). Which route runs depends on student demand, gathered from the [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/pal-resource-library|Introduction Survey]] in session 1, where you self-rate "Print Making" and name a preferred area of study. You will be told which route runs at least one week before A4, since A4 changes with the route.

| | **Poster** (default) | **Printmaking** |
|---|---|---|
| A4 task | Final poster, handmade or digital | Final linocut print |
| A4 marking focus | Hierarchy, typography, message, audience | Carving control, ink and registration, two-tone legibility |
| CS4 | Thumbnail set + poster analysis | Proof + annotation |

## Assessment

- **A4 (Attainment)** — **2026-12-23 (W17)**: final poster + reflection, handmade or digital. Announced 2026-12-16 (W16), the class immediately before. Marking focus: hierarchy, typography, message, audience.
- **CS4 (Course Skills)** — **2026-12-16 (W16)**: thumbnail set + poster analysis, in the same double that announces A4. Focus: hierarchy and audience.
- **EoT (End of Term)** — 2027-01-06 (W19): semester portfolio (four finals + process + reflections). Submitted, not sat.
- **A5 (Attainment)** — 2027-01-06 (W19): the sketchbook, submitted for its terminal attainment mark on the same day as the portfolio. Marking focus: ideas, experimentation, visual research, reflection, presentation.
