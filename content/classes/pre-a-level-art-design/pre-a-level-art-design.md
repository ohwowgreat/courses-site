# Pre A Level Art & Design

**"PAL" = Grade 10 Art & Design at BNDS** — a **non-exam, in-house studio course** with no external syllabus, graded under the BNDS A-Level Handbook ([[shared/bnds-assessment-framework]]). The G10 feeder year before [[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|CIE 9479 A Level]]. Delivery 2026-27: **one double session (two periods) every Wednesday**, ESL context. Record the naming equivalence: *PAL = Grade 10 Art* — the schedule masthead says "GRADE 10 ART".

⚠ **PAL 1 vs PAL 2 — inferred, not stated:** the evidence best supports *two semesters of the same G10 year* (PAL 1 = S1 autumn, PAL 2 = S2 spring: the schedule is "Grade 10 Art · Semester 1"; the Spring sheet says "Semester 2 is the studio semester… closing on a student exhibition in June. The Semester 2 map is not yet drawn"; PAL 2's two units are the first stages of exactly that arc). No document says it outright — **confirm before the wiki asserts it anywhere else.**

## Semester 1 (fully planned)

Four units, one final each, 18 Wednesday sessions (2026-09-02 → 2027-01-06) — detail in the [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/unit-plans/pal-semester-1-plan|Semester 1 Plan]]:

| Unit | Weeks | Spine | Final (Attainment) |
|---|---|---|---|
| U1 Drawing & the Visual Elements | W1–5 | "Observe · the elements" | A1 observational drawing, 2026-09-30 |
| U2 Photography | W7–10 | "Compose · intention" | A2 photographic series, 2026-11-04 |
| U3 Collage | W11–15 | "Combine · meaning" | A3 final collage, 2026-12-09 |
| U4 Graphic Design **/ Printmaking** | W16–19 (3 sessions) | "Communicate · audience" | A4 final poster, **2026-12-23** — or linocut, see [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/pal-s1-u4-printmaking-variant\|variant]] |
