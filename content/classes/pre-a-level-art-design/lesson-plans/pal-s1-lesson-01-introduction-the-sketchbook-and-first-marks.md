# PAL S1 Lesson 01: Introduction, the Sketchbook and First Marks

## Goal

By the end of this double you will know what the course is and how it is graded, you will have a working sketchbook and know the contract attached to it, and you will have started drawing — the session ends with marks on paper. The garlic drawing on the survey is your day-one baseline, made before any teaching.

## How it runs

**First half — the opening (~45 min):**

- **Welcome and the course (10 min)** — what the four units make (drawing → photography → collage → poster), and how the grade is built (Attainment 50 / Course Skills 20 / Learning Behaviour 10 / End of Term 20).
- **Introduction Survey (15 min)** — a two-page survey: favorites, preferred area of study, the 1–10 skill self-ratings, two open questions, then the garlic-bulb drawing ("draw this any way you like"). This is not a test; nobody fails a survey.
- **The sketchbook (15 min)** — receive or check your sketchbook. The contract, also posted on MB: **2–3 pages every week**, checked twice for a Course Skills mark (**SB1 Wed 10-21, SB2 Wed 12-02**), submitted whole at the end as **A5 (Wed 2027-01-06)**; plus a photo of the week's pages posted on MB after every session. Start your cover decoration now and finish it at home.
- **Questions (5 min).**

**Second half — U1·L1: the visual elements, starting with line (~45 min):**

- **The elements (10 min)** — the classroom element chart: line, shape, color, value, texture, space, form. One sentence each; this unit works through them from observation.
- **Blind contour (10 min)** — two 3-minute blind-contour drawings of a partner's hand or your own shoe: eyes on the subject, pen never lifts, no looking at the page. The point is looking, not likeness.
- **Continuous line (10 min)** — same subject, now looking allowed, still one unbroken line. Compare the pair: which one *looked* harder?
- **The mark grids (10 min)** — start *Drawing Exercises 1*: copy the hard-pencil (6H–2H) and soft-pencil (2B–6B) mark grids into your sketchbook. Started in class, finished at home.
- **Close (5 min)** — sketchbooks open on tables, one-minute walk-through; homework stated and posted on MB.

## Assessment

Nothing is graded this session. The survey is a baseline, not a mark. The sketchbook regime announced today is the standing notice for **A5** — the whole semester's book, submitted Wed 2027-01-06. Full register: [[classes/pre-a-level-art-design/assessments/pal-s1-assessments|PAL S1 Assessments]].
