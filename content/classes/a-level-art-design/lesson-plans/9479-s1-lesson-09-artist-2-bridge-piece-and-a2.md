## Goal

A short, dense lesson re-cut around the Juniors Days. Monday sets a second artist against the first and plans the piece that carries the student from study into development; Tuesday makes it; the gap finishes it. A2 reviews the whole of Unit 3, and the feedback day ends with one direction the student can defend.

## How it runs

- **Mon 11-02** (shared day, two beats) — Artist 2: rapid analysis against Artist 1, where they disagree about the theme; the bridge plan: composition, media, and what it carries from each artist — signed off before leaving.
- **Tue 11-03** — make the bridge piece: one sustained session, no restarts; gap brief: the finishing schedule, dated in the sketchbook.
- *(Juniors Days gap, six days — the finishing window.)*
- **Mon 11-09** — **A2 attainment.** Portfolio review in session: everything from Unit 3 (twelve experiments, media selection, scale trial, artist pages, bridge piece) against the published AO2 and AO3 criteria. Nothing added during review.
- **Tue 11-10** — A2 returned, banded; each student writes the one-sentence direction and defends it in a two-minute stand-up; Unit 4 preview.

## Assessment

**A2** (exploration and artist study milestone, AO2+AO3) sits Mon 2026-11-09 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]]. Artist 2 is chosen to argue with Artist 1: the comparison names a tension, not a similarity.
