## Goal

Nothing about the final outcome is left to the day itself. The composition is planned, solved small, and its palette and materials are settled before A3 reviews the whole development. Friday converts feedback into plan adjustments while there is still time for them to matter.

## How it runs

- **Mon 12-07** — the direction sentence and evaluation on the table; the final composition plan: full-size or scaled plan with structure, values and sequence of work marked; sign-off rota begins.
- **Tue 12-08** — "thumbnails that solve, not decorate": the plan tested small, one genuine alternative pushed and rejected on paper, the rejection annotated.
- **Wed 12-09** — "a palette argued from meaning": palette and materials settled, swatched, tested on the actual support, named for what they carry; self-check against the AO3 descriptors, in writing.
- **Thu 12-10** — **A3 attainment.** Portfolio review in session: compositions 1 and 2, targeted tests, the written evaluation, the final composition plan, against the published AO3 criteria. Nothing added during review.
- **Fri 12-11** — A3 returned, banded; feedback to plan adjustment: the plan physically amended, changes initialled; preview of the build run's shape and the studio booking.

## Assessment

**A3** (development milestone, AO3) sits Thu 2026-12-10 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]]. The bar for the plan: "complete enough that a capable stranger could begin the piece from it."
