## Goal

"Submission is a supervised craft, not a scramble." Monday photographs the outcome properly, because the photograph is the version the examiner sees. Tuesday sequences the whole journey so it reads in order. Wednesday cuts whatever does not earn its place and checks every AO. Thursday it goes in, and nothing is edited after. Friday looks back.

## How it runs

- **Mon 01-04** (shared day, two beats) — photograph the outcome: rig, light, shoot, detail passes; prepare the images: crop, correct, name, file to the template.
- **Tue 01-05** — sequencing logic: the exemplar's page order read as a story; sequence the portfolio, full run order laid out and walked once; peer walk: where does a stranger stumble.
- **Wed 01-06** (shared day, two beats) — edit and clean: cut what does not earn its place, log every cut; final AO check: all four descriptors, in writing, signed.
- **Thu 01-07** — **A4 attainment.** Final portfolio due and submitted in session. No edits after.
- **Fri 01-08** — submission acknowledged; what happens next (marking window, grades Wed 2027-01-20, return in W21); semester reflection written; the C3 bridge: what a Personal Investigation is, and the winter homework — three candidate themes with annotated references.

## Assessment

**A4** (final portfolio: the resolved outcome plus the complete supporting portfolio, sequenced and edited) sits Thu 2027-01-07 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]]. The sequence must read the whole journey in order without narration: theme, recording, exploration, artists, development, outcome.

## After the semester

W20 (Mon 01-11 →) is the exam window: no Art and Design classes, and the End of Term Exam format for a studio course remains an open item. W21 returns portfolios with one-to-one feedback Tue–Thu; grades and comments due Wed 2027-01-20. Winter vacation runs 2027-01-24 → 2027-02-21; the three C3 theme candidates open the Semester 2 consultation cycle on Mon 2027-02-22.
