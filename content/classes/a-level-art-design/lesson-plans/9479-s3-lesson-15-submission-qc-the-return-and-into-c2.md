## Goal

Close Component 3 and turn toward Component 2. The two W18 sessions assemble the PDF to the Cambridge screen limits and run submission QC so your work is board-ready before the break. Then your returned investigation is reviewed one to one, supervised gap repair catches anything the QC named, and a C2 preview points you toward the spring. The End-of-Term Final falls in the January window that follows.

## How it runs

- **W18 (about 2 sessions) — PDF assembly and submission QC.** Assembled to the Cambridge screen limits; every page must render; the QC is the last mark you can earn. These two sessions are the only slack in the tail, so attendance matters.
- **W19 — the return, 1:1.** Your complete investigation is returned and reviewed one to one; supervised gap repair for anything QC flagged; a **C2 preview** — what the Externally Set Assignment is and how the spring runs. Last teaching day is about Fri 2028-01-07.
- **W20 — the Final (EoT window, no teaching).** The 20% End-of-Term category is met by **the Final** — a teacher-marked assessment of your portfolio against the Cambridge criteria; no paper, no invigilation.
- **W21 — course closes.** C2 readiness briefing; grades and comments about Wed 2028-01-19.

## Key content: board-ready before the break, then into C2

- The two W18 QC sessions are the only slack in the tail — your submission is board-ready before the break, not after it.
- Your PDF must meet the Cambridge screen limits and every page must render — a submission that fails to open earns nothing.
- The **End of Term is the Final**, not a sit-down exam: this class sits no department finals. See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s3-assessments|register]].
- Component 3 is done; the drawing regimen and past-paper review over the vacation open Component 2 in [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|Semester 4]].
