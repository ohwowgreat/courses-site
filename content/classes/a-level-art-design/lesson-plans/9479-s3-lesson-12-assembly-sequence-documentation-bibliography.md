## Goal

Unit 6 is assembly, under AO4 Present. This week you sequence your complete investigation so the argument **reads in order to a stranger**, verify its documentation page by page — photographed, labeled, legible — and **integrate the bibliography into the body of the work**, not parked at the back. Whatever the sequence exposes as a gap, you repair before the critique.

## How it runs

- **Mon 12-06 — sequence plan.** The argument in order: where the investigation starts, turns and lands.
- **Tue 12-07 — documentation check.** Every page photographed, labeled and legible — the record survives without you.
- **Wed 12-08 — integrate the bibliography.** Into the body, not the back — sources appear where they did their work.
- **Thu 12-09 — studio: gap repair.** Close what the sequence exposed.
- **Fri 12-10 — sequencing check; CS9.** Where a stranger stalls, fix. CS9 marks the sequencing and documentation.

## Key content: it must read without you in the room

- Your investigation must **read in order without you in the room** — sequence is an AO4 mark, not a formality.
- A detached bibliography cost AO1 marks in the 90/100 C3 exemplar — integrate it into the body of the work.
- You verify documentation page by page now, so the submission QC in [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s3-lesson-15-submission-qc-the-return-and-into-c2|L15]] is a check, not a scramble.
- Next: the viva-rehearsal critique, where you defend every decision aloud — [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s3-lesson-13-the-viva-rehearsal-critique|L13]].
