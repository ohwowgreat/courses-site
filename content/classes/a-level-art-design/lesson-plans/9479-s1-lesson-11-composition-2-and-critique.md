## Goal

The alternative gets its fair chance. Composition 2 is the road not taken in Lesson 3's thumbnails, built with the same effort as the first so the comparison is honest. Then both are critiqued against the intention, and something changes because of what was heard.

## How it runs

- **Wed 11-18** — the rejected thumbnail from Lesson 3 on the table, its rejection note read back; compose composition 2 from the studies; sign-off rota.
- **Thu 11-19** — build composition 2: structure and main relationships in one day. The pace is deliberate.
- **Fri 11-20** — resolve composition 2; **CS8**: both compositions photographed and laid out side by side, collected.
- **Mon 11-23** — both compositions pinned with the intention between them; structured critique, full protocol, whole class in rounds: describe, evaluate against intention, one suggestion; maker speaks last, notes taken verbatim; each maker circles the two comments that matter.
- **Tue 11-24** — act on the critique: change something real on the stronger composition; annotate what changed and which comment caused it.

## Assessment

**CS8** (both compositions photographed and laid out) collects Fri 2026-11-20 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]]. Composition 2 must be genuinely the rejected alternative, not a variation of composition 1, and both must receive equal effort so the comparison is fair.
