## Goal

Bring outcome 2 to the same finish as outcome 1, then judge the pair. Outcome 2 is resolved and photographed **to the same standard** so the two can be judged together, and both are evaluated **in writing against the intention** — what the investigation found, not what it cost.

## How it runs

- **Mon–Tue 11-22/23 — studio: outcome 2.** Sustained through the reduced week; carried toward resolution.
- **Wed 11-24 — studio: outcome 2.** A4 (the complete Personal Investigation) is announced today.
- *(Thu–Fri — Seniors' Days, no teaching.)*
- **Mon–Tue 11-29/30 — studio: resolve outcome 2 (2-day run),** so the pair can be judged together — same finish, same care.
- **Wed 12-01 — photograph outcome 2.** Both outcomes documented to the same standard.
- **Thu–Fri 12-02/03 — evaluate both outcomes; CS8.** What the investigation found, in writing, against the intention. CS8 marks the paired evaluation.

## Key content: judged as a pair

- Both outcomes resolved and photographed to the **same standard** — an unequal pair reads as one finished piece and one draft.
- Both outcomes trace visibly back to the preparation and speak with **one voice** — two voices fail AO4.
- The evaluation judges the pair **against the intention**, naming what each found — this is AO4 evidence, not a diary.
