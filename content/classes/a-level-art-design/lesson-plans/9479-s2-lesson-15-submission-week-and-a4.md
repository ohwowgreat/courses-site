## Goal

This week you photograph, sequence, edit and submit the mid-point portfolio — everything from the locked theme to the resolved piece and the paper draft. On Monday you shoot the work properly, because the photograph is the version the examiner sees. On Tuesday you sequence the whole journey so it reads in order. On Wednesday you cut what does not earn its place and check every AO. Thursday is supervised QC, and on Friday it goes in — nothing edited after.

## How it runs

- **Mon 06-07 — photograph and prepare images.** Rig, light, shoot; detail passes on the resolved piece; then crop, correct, name and file the images to the template. **The photograph is the version the examiner sees.**
- **Tue 06-08 — sequence the mid-point portfolio.** Lay out the whole journey in order — theme, recording, exploration, artists, development, resolved piece, paper draft — so a stranger reads it without narration.
- **Wed 06-09 — edit and AO check.** Cut what does not earn its place, log every cut, then check all four AOs in writing: recording, exploration, development, presentation.
- **Thu 06-10 — final quality control, supervised.** Page by page against the checklist, in session: images clean, bibliography integrated, paper draft in place, sequence reading. Fixes only — nothing new starts.
- **Fri 06-11 — A4.** Mid-point portfolio due and submitted in session, marked against AO1–AO4. **No edits after.**

## Assessment

You sit **A4** (mid-point portfolio) on **Fri 2027-06-11**, marked against AO1–AO4 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s2-assessments|full register]]. It is the whole investigation so far, sequenced and edited: recording, exploration, artist pages, development, one resolved piece and the research paper draft. The sequence must read the whole journey in order without narration, and there are no edits after submission.

## After the semester

- **S17 (Mon 2027-06-13 → 19) — the Final.** The End-of-Term category (20%) is met by a teacher-marked assessment of your portfolio-so-far against the Cambridge criteria: no exam paper, no invigilation, no new task. The mid-point portfolio is returned one to one. The Final's format will be confirmed in class.
- **S18 (Mon 2027-06-20 → 26) — summer production briefing.** Targets are set piece by piece — the **largest uninterrupted C3 window** before the investigation resumes in [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan|Semester 3]] (autumn 2027, C3 part 2). Exact dates confirmed in class.

These spring and summer dates are provisional and may shift when the school publishes the calendar for that period.
