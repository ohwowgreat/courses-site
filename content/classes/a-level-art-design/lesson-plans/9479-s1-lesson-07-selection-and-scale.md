## Goal

The exploration becomes a commitment. The class critiques the experiment grids, each student selects their media for reasons they can write down, the selection is tested at real scale, and the scale trial's failures are fixed. The Stage 3 page then shows the exploration as reasoning, not as a swatch chart.

## How it runs

- **Mon 10-19** — grids up; group critique of the exploration, protocol on the board, maker speaks last; each student logs the two comments that changed their view.
- **Tue 10-20** — "a selection note that earns its choice": select media and document, chosen strips mounted beside the note; one-to-one sign-offs begin.
- **Wed 10-21 → Thu 10-22** — two-day run, one sheet at outcome scale: Wednesday the scale trial (chosen media at real size on the chosen composition), annotate what scale broke; Thursday refinement — fix what broke, nothing else, annotate the fix.
- **Fri 10-23** — the Stage 3 page as reasoning: experiments, decision, trial, refinement, in that order; **CS6 collected** with the selection note.

## Assessment

**CS6** (Stage 3 page + media selection note, written annotation) collects Fri 2026-10-23 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]]. The selection note names the chosen media in AO language: what this medium does to this theme. If the field trip takes Friday, CS6 collects Monday.
