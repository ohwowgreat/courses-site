## Goal

This week is the sustained build of final outcome 1 — one run, not a series of restarts — with a mid-production review placed while there is still time to change the work. Act on what the review names the same day. Monday also carries the notice for **A3**, the final research paper, 14 days ahead.

## How it runs

- **Mon 11-01 — outcome 1 build (3-day run opens).** One sustained run begins. **A3** (the final research paper) is announced today, 14 days out.
- **Tue–Wed 11-02/03 — studio: build outcome 1.** The run continues; execute the plan from CS5.
- **Thu 11-04 — mid-production review; CS6.** CS6 is this review, marked.
- **Fri 11-05 — studio: act on the review.** Today, not later — the point of a mid-production review is that the work is still movable.

## Key content: one sustained run, reviewed in time

- Outcome 1 is built as **one sustained run**, not a sequence of fresh starts — continuity is what carries AO3 into AO4.
- The mid-production review lands **while there is still time to change the work**; a review after the work is fixed changes nothing.
- Act on the review **the same day** — CS6 marks the review, but the work is what proves you used it.
- The build resolves and is photographed in [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s3-lesson-09-resolving-outcome-1-and-the-final-paper-text|L09]], alongside the paper's final text.
