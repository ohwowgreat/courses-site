## Goal

Close exploration and carry it into development. Artist 2 gives you a different angle on the same question; the bridge piece moves study into development; and a scale trial at the supervised-test dimensions surfaces problems now, not in the test room. Your exploration pages should reason rather than display swatches. Friday is A2 — the whole unit, marked across AO2 and AO3.

## How it runs

- **Mon 03-20 — artist 2: analysis and response.** Two angles, one question — answer artist 2 in your work, not just by citing them.
- **Tue 03-21 — studio: bridge piece; CS5.** From study into development. CS5 checks the artist pages and the bridge piece.
- **Wed 03-22 — studio: scale trial.** At the test scale — fix what broke while there is still time.
- **Thu 03-23 — exploration pages.** Reasoning, not a swatch chart — why these media, for this question.
- **Fri 03-24 — A2: exploration and artist study.** Experiments, media selection, two artists with visible responses, bridge piece and scale trial, marked together across AO2+AO3.

## Key content: two angles, and no surprises left for the test

- ◆ Citation without response earns nothing — the response is the evidence, for both artists.
- ◆ The scale trial happens at the dimensions the supervised test will use — surprises belong here, not in the test room.
- The bridge piece is the hinge from exploration into development; A2 marks the whole unit as one.
- Development — the tightest unit — and the content close at A3 follow in [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s4-lesson-06-development-and-a3-the-content-close|L06]].
