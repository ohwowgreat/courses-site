# S4 Lesson 07: The 15-Hour Supervised Test

These dates are provisional and may shift when the school publishes the exam calendar for this period.

## Goal

This is the exam proper. The outcome you planned at A3 is **realised inside the 15-hour supervised test** under exam conditions — no additions, no access between sittings, and **nothing invented under supervision**. The teaching days before the test go to refinement and rehearsal, never to new content, because content closed at A3.

## How it runs

- **The elastic window.** The teaching days of the week before go to **refinement and rehearsal** of your plan — not new work.
- **The 15-hour supervised test.** Your plan realised under exam conditions. The test runs Mon 2028-04-10 → Fri 04-14, five sittings of 3 hours. An alternative timing runs around Qingming (Tue 04-04) as four sittings of 3h45, with the whole schedule pulling forward one week. Which form applies will be confirmed in class.
- **Between sittings — secure storage.** Your work is stored securely and documented: **no access, no additions, no exceptions.**

## Key content: nothing invented under supervision

- ◆ **Nothing is invented under supervision** — the plan you marked at A3 is both your safety and your mark.
- Storage between sittings is **secure and documented**: the integrity of the test depends on it.
- Assembly of the submission package follows in [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s4-lesson-08-assembly-and-a4-the-submission-package|L08]].
