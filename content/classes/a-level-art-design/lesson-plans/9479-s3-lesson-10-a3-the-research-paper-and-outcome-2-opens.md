## Goal

The research paper is done, and the second outcome begins. Monday is **A3** — your final research paper, marked against the C3 written criteria and submitted with a one-to-one review. Then outcome 2 opens: it must **answer the first, not repeat it**, and you plan it before you build it so nothing is left to the day. The two outcomes stay inside one investigation, in one voice.

## How it runs

- **Mon 11-15 — A3: final research paper.** Submit it with a 1:1 review. It is marked against the C3 written criteria — every claim evidenced in your portfolio.
- **Tue 11-16 — plan outcome 2.** Decide composition, palette, and materials; your plan states how it answers outcome 1.
- **Wed–Thu 11-17/18 — studio: outcome 2 build opens (2-day run).** You start while the first is fresh, so the pair reads as one investigation.
- **Fri 11-19 — studio: outcome 2 continues; CS7.** CS7 marks your outcome 2 plan and progress.

## Key content: two outcomes, one voice

- ◆ Both outcomes must trace visibly back to your preparation — **an outcome from nowhere fails AO4 regardless of quality**.
- Outcome 2 **answers** outcome 1 — a different question inside the same investigation, not a repeat.
- ◆ Both outcomes speak with **one voice**; two voices fail AO4.
- A4 (the complete investigation) is announced Wed 2027-11-24 during the outcome 2 build — [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s3-lesson-11-outcome-2-resolved-and-both-evaluated|L11]].
