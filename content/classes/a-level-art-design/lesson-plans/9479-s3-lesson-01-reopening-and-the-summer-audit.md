## Goal

The Personal Investigation reopens where your summer production left it. By Friday every piece you made over the summer is out, annotated, and judged honestly against the target set for it in June — **met, missed, or missing** — because the audit marks against the June targets, not against effort. What you set in writing in June is what counts now. You add nothing to the investigation this week; the job is to know exactly where it stands before you set goals against it in [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s3-lesson-02-the-action-plan-and-development-resumes|L02]].

## How it runs

- **Wed 09-01 — course reopening and the summer audit brief.** Lay out all your summer output at once, against the June targets. The audit standard: a piece counts only if it is present *and* annotated against a named target. The four AOs are recalled in C3 language, because the whole investigation — not this term's work alone — is what the audit measures.
- **Thu 09-02 — one-to-one reviews begin.** Defend your work piece by piece. Each piece is marked **met, missed, or missing** against its June target — not against how hard it was to make.
- **Fri 09-03 — reviews continue; CS1.** Every summer piece is closed out as met/missed/missing; the gaps are named in writing. CS1 *is* this audit, marked piece by piece — the raw material for next week's action plan.

## Key content: the audit standard

- ◆ The audit marks against the **June targets, not against effort** — what was set in writing in June is what counts in September.
- Every summer piece must be present, annotated and judged against a named target; **unannotated work fails the audit standard and costs AO1**.
- Your summer reading reflections open **research paper draft 2** in [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s3-unit-4-development-second-pass|Unit 4]].
- The audit becomes the semester's contract: the written, dated, signed action plan you build in [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s3-lesson-02-the-action-plan-and-development-resumes|L02]], which governs every week to the December submission.
