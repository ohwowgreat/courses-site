# 9479 S4 Unit 3: Exploration & Artist Study

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|Semester 4 Plan]] · Unit 3 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-2-recording|Unit 2]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-4-development|Unit 4]] →

## What this unit does

The exploration / artist-study stage at exam pace, across AO2 Explore and AO3 Develop. A1 feedback becomes targets; **eight** experiments (compressed from the C3 twelve, drawn from the student's proven selections) run to a written keep-or-change decision; two artists are analyzed and answered in visible work within single sessions; and the bridge piece and a scale trial — at the supervised-test dimensions — carry study toward development. Closes with **A2**, marked across AO2 and AO3.

## Dates & span

Weeks S4–S5 (projected 2028-03-12 → ~2028-03-25), 10 sessions. **All S4 dates are projected** — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|semester plan]].

## Sequence of work

| Code | Session | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| U3·L1 | Feedback; plan the experiments | Targets first, then risk |
| U3·L2–L3 | Studio: media experiments (2-day session) | Eight experiments; what carries the question |
| U3·L4 | Artist 1: analysis and response in one pass | Analyse, then answer in work |
| U3·L5 | Artist 1 response work | Influence in the work. |
| U3·L6 | Artist 2: analysis and response | Two angles, one question |
| U3·L7 | Studio: bridge piece | From study into development |
| U3·L8 | Studio: scale trial | At the test scale; fix what broke |
| U3·L9 | Exploration pages | Reasoning, not a swatch chart |
| U3·L10 | **A2: Exploration and artist study** | The whole unit, marked together |

## Assessment

Gates on **A2, Fri 2028-03-24** — exploration and artist study (experiments, media selection, two artists with visible work responses, bridge piece, scale trial), marked against AO2+AO3. CS tasks: **CS4** Fri 2028-03-17 (experiment documentation + artist 1 response), **CS5** Tue 2028-03-21 (artist pages + bridge piece check). at U3·L5 (Fri 2028-03-17) — before A2 sits, on the tight compressed run. See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s4-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- ◆ Media choices carry conceptual logic **even at exam pace**.
- ◆ Citation without response earns nothing — **the response is the evidence**.
- The scale trial happens at the dimensions the supervised test will use: surprises belong here, not in the test room.
