# 9479 S1 Unit 5: Final Outcome & Portfolio

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-1-plan|Semester 1 Plan]] · Unit 5 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s1-unit-4-development|Unit 4]]

## What this unit does

The final-outcome / portfolio stage, under AO4 Present. The final outcome is built in one protected continuous run (four build days, a mid-production review with real time left, three resolve days) governed by the Lesson 13 plan, then submission week treats assembly as "a supervised craft": examiner-grade photography, sequencing the whole journey, an edit with a cut log, and A4 — the final portfolio, marked across AO1–AO4, closing the semester.

## Dates & span

Mon 2026-12-14 → Fri 2027-01-08 (W16 → W19), with the winter break (W18) between the declared finish and supervised assembly. The late-work cutoff (≈Fri 2027-01-01) passes during the break.

## Lessons

- [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s1-lesson-14-the-final-outcome|Lesson 14: The Final Outcome]] — four-day build (underdrawing → mid-stages → production → detail), mid-production review while change is still possible, three-day resolve run ending in a written declared finish.
- [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s1-lesson-15-submission-week-and-a4|Lesson 15: Submission Week and A4]] — photograph, sequence, edit with cut log, final AO check, submission Thursday, semester reflection and the C3 bridge Friday.

## Assessment

Gates on **A4, Thu 2027-01-07** — final portfolio (the resolved outcome plus the complete supporting portfolio, sequenced and edited), marked against AO1–AO4; no edits after submission. Announced Mon 2026-12-21 — a 17-day window covering the break. Marking window Thu 2027-01-07 → Wed 2027-01-20 (nine working days); grades and comments due Wed 2027-01-20. One CS task: **CS10** (mid-production review reflection), Fri 2026-12-18 — direct practice for A4. See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- The build run is the semester's longest and is protected: "any lost day here has no home elsewhere: the calendar has zero spare days." Nothing new enters the room during the run; deviation from the plan is a conference, not a drift.
- The declared finish (Wed 2026-12-23) is real: the break is rest, no homework, and the portfolio is assembled only in supervised January sessions.
- Submission craft: the photograph is the version the examiner sees; the sequence reads theme → recording → exploration → artists → development → outcome without narration; at least one page is cut for not earning its place and the cut is logged (evidence of editorial judgment if a mark is queried).
- Friday closes with the C3 bridge: winter homework is three candidate Personal Investigation themes with annotated references, opening the Semester 2 consultation cycle on Mon 2027-02-22.
