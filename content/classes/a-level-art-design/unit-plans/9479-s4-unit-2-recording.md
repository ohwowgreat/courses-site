# 9479 S4 Unit 2: Recording

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|Semester 4 Plan]] · Unit 2 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-1-foundations-on-the-question|Unit 1]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-3-exploration-and-artist-study|Unit 3]] →

## What this unit does

The recording stage at **exam pace**, under AO1 Record. An intention is stated on the chosen question and the reference shoot is planned in the same session; self-generated reference is shot and drawn within the same week; one observed study is opened and sustained without restarting; annotation ties every page to the question; and the recording pages are built as the work happens — there is no later. Closes with **A1**, marked against AO1.

## Dates & span

Weeks S2–S3 (projected 2028-02-27 → ~2028-03-11), 10 sessions. **All S4 dates are projected** — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|semester plan]].

## Key content

- ◆ All reference is self-generated — **"nothing found, nothing borrowed" does not relax under exam pressure**.
- The sustained study shows duration despite the pace.
- Homework stays capped at 30 minutes: exam pace does not mean exam-season homework inflation.
