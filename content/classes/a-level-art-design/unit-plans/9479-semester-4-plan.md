# 9479 Semester 4 Plan

> [[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · Semester 4 of 4 ([[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-course-map|course map]]) · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan|S3 C3 part 2]] · course closes

**Component 2 (Externally Set Assignment) plus all-component submission, G12, spring 2027–28.** One session every teaching day, from Mon 2028-02-21 to the content close on Fri 2028-03-31, then the mock window, the 15-hour supervised test, assembly and the board handover: **40 coded sessions** (30 in the six-week core, 10 in assembly/board) plus the elastic window and the test.

The dates below (weeks S1–S12, and A1–A4) are projected and may shift when the school publishes the spring 2027–28 calendar. A4 and the supervised test move with the mock window.
