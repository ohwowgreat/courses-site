# A Level Art & Design · Semester 4 Plan

> [[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · Semester 4 of 4 ([[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-course-map|course map]]) · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan|S3 C3 part 2]] · course closes

**Component 2 (Externally Set Assignment) plus the all-component submission, G12, spring 2027-28.** There is one session every teaching day, from Mon 2028-02-21 to the content close on Fri 2028-03-31, followed by the mock window, the 15-hour supervised test, assembly and the board handover: **40 coded sessions** (30 in the six-week core, 10 in assembly/board) plus the elastic window and the test. This is the tightest semester in the course, built around the Cambridge set question.

> [!note] About the dates
> All dates below are provisional and may shift once the school publishes the 2027-28 spring calendar. A4 and the supervised test in particular move with the mock window.
