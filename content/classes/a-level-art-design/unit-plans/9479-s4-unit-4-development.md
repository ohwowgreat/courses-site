# 9479 S4 Unit 4: Development

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|Semester 4 Plan]] · Unit 4 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-3-exploration-and-artist-study|Unit 3]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-5-supervised-test-and-submission|Unit 5]] →

## What this unit does

The development stage, under AO3 Develop closing into AO4 Present — the tightest unit in the course. Final-piece candidates are composed from the student's own studies (the test realises one of them); they are compared on a **draft comparison page** with the rejections articulated in writing; a final composition plan is fixed so nothing is invented under supervision; and every supporting study is completed and the mounted order decided. **Content closes at A3**; nothing new is taught after it. The one taught development week is widened only by the elastic window (reading B, or any earlier CNY opening).

## Dates & span

Week S6 plus the elastic window (projected 2028-03-26 → 2028-03-31 for the coded sessions; the S7 window adds up to four days on reading B). **All S4 dates are projected** — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|semester plan]].

| Code | Session | Focus |

| U4·L2 | Draft comparison page | Rejections articulated in writing |
| U4·L3 | Final composition plan | Nothing invented under supervision |
| U4·L4 | Supporting studies completed | Every gap closed |
| U4·L5 | **A3: Complete supporting studies — content close** | Comparison page, plan, mounted order. |
| *Elastic (S7)* | Reading B window | All four teaching days go to refinement and rehearsal |

Gates on **A3, Fri 2028-03-31** — complete supporting studies (draft comparison page with articulated rejections, final composition plan, mounted order), marked against AO3+AO4; **this is the content close — nothing new is taught after it**. at U4·L5 (Fri 2028-03-31, with the A3 sitting), so the notice covers the mock window wherever it lands. One CS task: **CS6** Thu 2028-03-30 (composition critique response + draft comparison page). See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s4-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- ◆ **The draft comparison page with articulated rejections earns top AO4 in the exemplars** — it is the unit's center of gravity.
- The final plan is complete enough that the test is execution, not invention.
- The compression is deliberate and flagged: one taught development week, widened only by the elastic window. Every gained day lands here.
