# S3 Unit 5: Final Outcomes

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan|Semester 3 Plan]] · Unit 5 · 3rd of 4 this semester · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s3-unit-4-development-second-pass|Unit 4]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s3-unit-6-assembly-and-submission|Unit 6]] →

## What this unit does

You build both final outcomes and complete the research paper, under AO3 Develop and AO4 Present. You plan each outcome in advance — composition, palette, materials — so nothing is left to the day itself, then build it across merged multi-day runs with a mid-production review while change is still possible, and resolve and photograph it to examiner standard. The two outcomes stay inside one investigation with one voice. The research paper reaches final text, with every claim pointing at real work.

The unit carries **two attainments**: **A2**, the whole-investigation mock review, and **A3**, the final research paper.

## Dates & span

Weeks W8–W14 (2027-10-17 → about 2027-12-04), 28 sessions, around the W9 Seniors' Days (no teaching) and the reduced W13. These dates are projected and may shift when the school publishes the calendar for this period.

## Assessment

Two attainments gate this unit.

- **A2, Thu 2027-10-21** — mock portfolio review of the whole investigation to date, marked against AO1–AO4. It also serves as portfolio application material.
- **A3, Mon 2027-11-15** — the final research paper, marked against the C3 written criteria.

CS tasks:
- **CS5**, Tue 2027-10-19 — outcome 1 plan
- **CS6**, Thu 2027-11-04 — outcome 1 mid-production review
- **CS7**, Fri 2027-11-19 — outcome 2 plan and progress
- **CS8**, Fri 2027-12-03 — evaluation of both outcomes

These dates are projected and may shift when the school publishes the calendar. See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s3-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- ◆ Both outcomes must trace visibly back to the preparation — **an outcome from nowhere fails AO4 regardless of quality**.
- Both outcomes speak with one voice; two voices fail AO4.
- The paper's specialist language is exact, and every claim is evidenced in the portfolio.
- The A2 mock review is sequenced as a real submission — it is both a dress rehearsal and an application asset.
