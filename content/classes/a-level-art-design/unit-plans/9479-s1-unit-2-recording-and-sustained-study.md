# 9479 S1 Unit 2: Recording & Sustained Study

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-1-plan|Semester 1 Plan]] · Unit 2 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s1-unit-1-foundations-on-the-theme|Unit 1]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s1-unit-3-exploration-and-artist-study|Unit 3]] →

## What this unit does

The recording / sustained-study stage of the pipeline, entirely under AO1 Record. The theme becomes a written investigation (intention, mind map, thumbnails with annotated rejections), gets its spine — one long observed study carried across two days without restarting — and everything recorded becomes pages an examiner could read. The unit closes with A1, the semester's first attainment and first use of the attainment review protocol.

## Dates & span

Tue 2026-09-15 → Sat 2026-10-10 (W3 → W6), split by the National Day break; includes the Sun 2026-09-20 make-up day and the Sat 2026-10-10 make-up day (Sports Day afternoon).

## Lessons

- [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s1-lesson-03-intention-and-composition-planning|Lesson 03: Intention and Composition Planning]] — written intention, mind map, ≥6 thumbnails, one composition chosen, rejections annotated ("the top band habit").
- [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s1-lesson-04-the-sustained-study|Lesson 04: The Sustained Study]] — Stage 1 page; the sustained study across a genuine two-day run ("stamina is the content").
- [[classes/a-level-art-design/lesson-plans/9479-s1-lesson-05-recording-pages-and-a1|Lesson 05: Recording Pages and A1]] — own photography, studies brought to one standard, recording pages sequenced; AO1 self-check; A1 sat and fed back.

## Assessment

Gates on **A1, Fri 2026-10-09** — recording milestone (intention, Stage 1 pages, sustained study, supports) marked against AO1; feeds the mid-term grades due Thu 2026-10-29. CS tasks inside the unit: **CS2** Fri 2026-09-18 (intention + mind map), **CS3** Thu 2026-09-24 (sustained-study walked check), **CS4** Wed 2026-09-30 (recording pages build check) — each direct practice for A1. See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s1-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- Sustained-study media are assigned per student from the W1 diagnostic — the prescription rule doing real work.
- The intention drafting frame (*I am investigating / because / I will record*) scaffolds the ESL register, then is struck: the voice ends up the student's.
- The AO1 self-check before A1 is the calibration habit the whole course leans on: the student's own banding should land within one band of the teacher's; self-bandings are collected and the gap tracked.
- The holiday split is deliberate: pages built before the break, review after it; holiday homework is primary-source sketchbook recording.
