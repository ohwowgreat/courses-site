# 9479 S4 Unit 1: Foundations on the Question

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|Semester 4 Plan]] · Unit 1 of 5 · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-2-recording|Unit 2]] →

## What this unit does

Opens the Component 2 Externally Set Assignment on the Cambridge paper itself, distributed over the vacation. The paper is read closely and each question named for what it permits and quietly forbids; the choice is matched to **proven strengths from C1 and C3** (evidenced by the drawing regimen and past work, not hopes); two candidate questions are defended one to one; and one question is **locked in writing with a rationale** by Friday — the whole semester runs on it. This is the exam.

## Dates & span

Week S1 (projected Mon 2028-02-21 → 2028-02-25), 5 sessions. **All S4 dates are projected**; any weeks gained from an earlier CNY opening go to Unit 4, not to a longer Unit 1 — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|semester plan]].

## Sequence of work

| Code | Session | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| U1·L1 | Course reopening and the ESA briefing | Read the Cambridge paper together. This is the exam |
| U1·L2 | Question analysis | What each question permits, and what it quietly forbids |
| U1·L3 | Strengths audit | Match questions to proven strengths from C1 and C3, not to hopes |
| U1·L4 | Question shortlist, one to one | Two candidate questions defended |
| U1·L5 | Question locked with rationale | In writing. with the lock |

## Assessment

No attainment gates this unit — the first, **A1**, sits Fri 2028-03-10 in [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s4-unit-2-recording|Unit 2]] and is. One CS task: **CS1** Fri 2028-02-25 (question choice with written rationale, locked one to one). See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s4-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- ◆ The strengths audit runs on **what the portfolio shows, not on preference** — the exam rewards what the student can already do at pace.
- The question is locked in writing by Friday with a rationale grounded in evidence.
- The winter regimen closes here: past-paper review notes and drawing-regimen evidence feed the audit directly.
- Confirm the ESA release with the exams officer; distribute the paper the moment it publishes (during the vacation if possible).
