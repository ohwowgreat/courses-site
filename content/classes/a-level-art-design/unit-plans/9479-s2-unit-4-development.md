# 9479 S2 Unit 4: Development

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan|Semester 2 Plan]] · Unit 4 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s2-unit-3-exploration-and-artist-study|Unit 3]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s2-unit-5-mid-point-portfolio|Unit 5]] →

## What this unit does

The development stage, under AO3 Develop, with the research paper drafted in parallel. Two competing compositions are built to the same resolved standard (the chosen direction and the genuinely rejected alternative), both critiqued against the intention; targeted tests answer only what the critique left unresolved; the final direction is chosen with the rejection articulated in writing; and the written evaluation and Stage 6 page assemble the decision trail. Research paper **draft 1** is completed from the contextual writing against a teacher-set outline. Closes with **A3**, marked against AO3 with written/practical integration in view.

## Dates & span

Weeks S10–S14 (Mon 2027-04-25 → ~2027-05-29), 22 sessions, spanning the May Day reduced week (S11). Provisional spring dates — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan|semester plan]].

## Key content

- Two compositions reach the same resolved standard, so the comparison is fair; composition 2 is not built to fail.
- ◆ **A draft comparison page with articulated rejections earns top AO4 in the exemplars**, and it is built here.
- Draft 1 grows from the artist writing, not bolted on: argue before you polish the prose.
- Book studio rooms for S12–S14 early — the school AS/A2 exam season may take spaces.
