# 9479 S3 Unit 1: Review & Goal Setting

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan|Semester 3 Plan]] · Unit 1 · 1st of 4 this semester · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s3-unit-4-development-second-pass|Unit 4]] →

## What this unit does

Reopens the Personal Investigation after the summer production window. The whole summer output is laid out and audited honestly against the targets set in June — met, missed, missing — and defended one to one, piece by piece. The audit is converted into goals that name pages, pieces and dates, and the unit closes with a **written, dated, signed action plan** that governs the semester to the December submission.

> **S3 skips U2 and U3.** Recording and exploration were completed in Semester 2; the pipeline resumes here at review, then jumps straight to **U4, the development second pass**. The C3 unit numbering (U1, U4, U5, U6) is kept from the pipeline stages.

## Dates & span

Weeks W1–W2 (projected Wed 2027-09-01 → ~2027-09-08), 5 sessions. **All S3 dates are projected** — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan|semester plan]].

## Sequence of work

| Code | Session | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| U1·L1 | Course reopening and the summer audit brief | All of it, against the targets |
| U1·L2 | One to one reviews begin | Defend the work, piece by piece |
| U1·L3 | One to one reviews continue | Met, missed, missing |
| U1·L4 | Goals from the audit | Pages, pieces, dates |
| U1·L5 | Written action plan, teacher directed | Dated, signed, runs to submission |

## Assessment

No attainment gates this unit — no attainment falls in the first two teaching weeks; the first, **A1**, sits Thu 2027-10-14 in [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s3-unit-4-development-second-pass|Unit 4]]. One CS task: **CS1** Fri 2027-09-03 (summer portfolio audit against the set targets, marked piece by piece). See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s3-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- ◆ The audit marks against the **June targets, not against effort** — what was set in writing in June is what counts in September.
- Every summer piece must be present, annotated and judged against a named target; unannotated work fails the audit standard and costs AO1.
- The action plan is the semester's contract: written, dated, signed and specific.
- The summer reading reflections open **research paper draft 2** in Unit 4.
