# 9479 Semester 3 Plan

> [[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · Semester 3 of 4 ([[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-course-map|course map]]) · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan|S2 C3 part 1]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan|S4 C2 + submission]] →

**Component 3 (Personal Investigation), part 2, G12, autumn 2027-28.** One session every teaching day, Wed 2027-09-01 → ~Fri 2028-01-07: **71 coded sessions.** This semester picks up where the summer work left off — audit and goal-setting, a second development pass, both final outcomes and the research paper, then assembly and internal submission of the complete investigation at A4.

> [!note] Dates may change
> All dates below (including A1–A4) are projected and may shift once the school publishes the 2027-28 calendar. Any changes will be confirmed in class.

This semester carries units **U1, U4, U5, U6**. Recording and exploration (U2/U3) are not repeated — development resumes directly as U4, the "second pass," building final-outcome candidates from your summer work.
