# 9479 S2 Unit 2: Recording & Sustained Study

[[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan|Semester 2 Plan]] · Unit 2 of 5 · ← [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s2-unit-1-foundations-on-the-investigation|Unit 1]] | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-s2-unit-3-exploration-and-artist-study|Unit 3]] →

## What this unit does

The recording / sustained-study stage, entirely under AO1 Record, carried in C3 specialist language. Recording pages are composed so the thinking is visible to a stranger; two observed studies are each sustained across a genuine two-day run without restarting; annotation is critical (what the study taught about the theme, not what happened); and self-generated photographic reference is shot and annotated. The unit closes the recording sequence and carries **A1**, the semester's first attainment.

## Dates & span

Weeks S4–S5 (Mon 2027-03-14 → ~2027-03-27), 10 sessions. Provisional spring dates — see the [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan|semester plan]].

## Sequence of work

| Code | Session | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| U2·L1 | Compose the Stage 1 page | Thinking visible to a stranger |
| U2·L2–L3 | Studio: sustained study 1 (2-day session) | One study sustained, not restarted |
| U2·L4 | Critical annotation | What the study taught about the theme |
| U2·L5 | **A1: Recording milestone** | Everything recorded so far, marked against AO1 |
| U2·L6 | Feedback and targets | Three specific actions from the feedback |
| U2·L7–L8 | Studio: sustained study 2 (2-day session) | Pushed past the first |
| U2·L9 | Photography and annotation | Own reference, annotated for what is seen |
| U2·L10 | Studio: build the recording pages | Sequenced so AO1 reads without explanation |

## Assessment

Gates on **A1, Fri 2027-03-19** — recording milestone (approved intention, Stage 1 pages, the first sustained study, supporting recording), marked against AO1 with C3 specialist language in view. One CS task inside the unit: **CS3** Fri 2027-03-26 (recording pages build check, consolidating the A1 feedback actions). See the [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s2-assessments|full register]].

## Key content

- Both sustained studies must show duration — layered decisions, not single sittings.
- ◆ Descriptive annotation caps AO1 in every exemplar; **critical reflection on progress is the top-band behavior**.
- All reference is self-generated ("nothing found, nothing borrowed").
- A1 feedback is converted into three named actions and visibly acted on by CS3.
