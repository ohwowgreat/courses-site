# 9479 Course Map

> [[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · the whole two-year course at a glance

**C1 → C3 (across two semesters) → C2**, closing on a **linear A Level entry in June 2028** (confirmed). **All four semesters now have day-level schedules and unit plans:**

| Semester | When | Component | Cohort | Plan |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| S1 | autumn 2026 | C1 Portfolio | G11 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-1-plan\|S1 Plan]] · 5 units · 15 lesson plans |
| S2 | spring 2027 | C3 Personal Investigation, part 1 | G11 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan\|S2 Plan]] · 5 units |
| S3 | autumn 2027 | C3 part 2 → internal submission | G12 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan\|S3 Plan]] · 4 units |
| S4 | spring 2028 | C2 Externally Set Assignment → **all-component submission** | G12 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan\|S4 Plan]] · 5 units |

**Dates:** S1 follows the ingested [[calendar|2026-27 calendar]]. S2 uses real spring-2027 weekdays but is **provisional** (no spring calendar ingested); S3 and S4 dates are **projected** until the 2027-28 calendar publishes.

**What linear entry means here:** all three components are entered in the single June 2028 series, so there is no separate AS award and no carry-forward step. Spring 2027 therefore carries **no Cambridge deadline** — the first and only submission is ≈ end April 2028, where C1, C2 and C3 land together (S4 wk 11).

**Design constants:** the eight-stage portfolio pipeline every semester; 4 Attainments + 8–10 Course Skills tasks per semester; homework every week (sketchbook / written annotation / reflection, ≤30 min); theme control tightens each component — C1 teacher menu → C3 student-proposed and locked in writing → C2 Cambridge-set with the teacher shortlisting 2–3 questions per student.

## S2 — spring 2027 · C3 part 1 (theme → mid-point portfolio)

Opens Mon 2027-02-22. Three weeks of consultation, then the theme is locked and the research paper drafts alongside the visual work. Dates are real 2027 weekdays but **provisional** (no spring calendar ingested).

| Code | Date | Milestone | AO |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | Fri 2027-03-19 | Recording milestone, in C3 specialist language | AO1 |

| A3 | Fri 2027-05-28 | Development + research paper, first full draft | AO3 + integration |
| A4 | Fri 2027-06-11 | **C3 mid-point portfolio** | AO1–AO4 |

Other fixed points: theme **locked in writing** by week 3; Qingming mock week floats (early–mid April, sketchbook brief only); the research paper outline is issued mid-semester and its bibliography is **integrated into the body of work** (a detached bibliography cost AO1 marks in the 90/100 exemplar); the semester closes on a **summer production briefing** — the largest uninterrupted C3 window. - **Week 1:** confirm the ESA paper release with the exams officer and distribute immediately; the teacher shortlists 2–3 questions per student and the **choice is locked by Friday**.
- **The 15-hour supervised test runs in Qingming mock week**, unless the school sets a dedicated window — it does not fit the daily timetable, so book studio and invigilation the day the mock dates are announced.
- **≈ end April 2028: coursework marks and samples to Cambridge** — on the linear entry, C1, C2 and C3 all land at this single deadline. The June 2028 timetable publishes ≈ Oct 2027.
- Two artists is a calibration point, not a preference: single-artist submissions sat in the middle and low exemplar bands.
