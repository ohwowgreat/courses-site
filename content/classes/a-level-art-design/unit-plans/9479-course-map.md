# A Level Art & Design · Course Map

> [[classes/a-level-art-design/a-level-art-design|A Level Art & Design]] · the whole two-year course at a glance

The course runs **C1 → C3 (across two semesters) → C2**, closing on a **linear A Level entry in June 2028**.

| Semester | When | Component | Cohort | Plan |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| S1 | autumn 2026 | C1 Portfolio | G11 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-1-plan\|S1 Plan]] · 5 units |
| S2 | spring 2027 | C3 Personal Investigation, part 1 | G11 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan\|S2 Plan]] · 5 units |
| S3 | autumn 2027 | C3 part 2 → internal submission | G12 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-3-plan\|S3 Plan]] · 4 units |
| S4 | spring 2028 | C2 Externally Set Assignment → **all-component submission** | G12 | [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-4-plan\|S4 Plan]] · 5 units |

**Dates:** S1 follows the [[calendar|2026-27 calendar]]. S2, S3 and S4 dates are provisional and may shift when the school publishes the calendars for spring 2027 and 2027-28.

**What linear entry means here:** all three components are entered in the single June 2028 series, so there is no separate AS award and no carry-forward step. Spring 2027 therefore carries **no Cambridge deadline** — the first and only submission is ≈ end April 2028, where C1, C2 and C3 land together (S4 wk 11).

**Design constants:** the eight-stage portfolio pipeline every semester; 4 Attainments + 8–10 Course Skills tasks per semester; homework every week (sketchbook / written annotation / reflection, ≤30 min); theme control tightens each component — C1 teacher menu → C3 student-proposed and locked in writing → C2 Cambridge-set with the teacher shortlisting 2–3 questions per student.

## S2 — spring 2027 · C3 part 1 (theme → mid-point portfolio)

Opens Mon 2027-02-22. You have three weeks of consultation, then the theme is locked and the research paper drafts alongside the visual work.

| Code | Date | Milestone | AO |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | Fri 2027-03-19 | Recording milestone, in C3 specialist language | AO1 |
| A2 | Fri 2027-04-23 | Exploration + Artist 1 | AO2+AO3 |
| A3 | Fri 2027-05-28 | Development + research paper, first full draft | AO3 + integration |
| A4 | Fri 2027-06-11 | **C3 mid-point portfolio** | AO1–AO4 |

Other fixed points: your theme is **locked in writing** by week 3; the Qingming mock week falls early–mid April (sketchbook brief only); the research paper outline is issued mid-semester and its bibliography must be **integrated into the body of work**, not detached (a detached bibliography loses AO1 marks); the semester closes on a **summer production briefing** — the largest uninterrupted C3 window. Full detail: [[classes/a-level-art-design/unit-plans/9479-semester-2-plan|S2 Plan]] · [[classes/a-level-art-design/assessments/9479-s2-assessments|S2 Assessments]].

## S4 — spring 2028 · C2 + submission

| Code | Date | Milestone | AO |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | Fri 2028-03-10 | Recording — intention, shoots, sustained studies | AO1 |
| A2 | Fri 2028-03-24 | Experiments + **two artists** + bridge piece + scale trial | AO2+AO3 |

| A4 | Fri 2028-04-21 | Complete C2 submission package | — |

- **Week 1:** the ESA paper is released and distributed; the teacher shortlists 2–3 questions per student and your **choice is locked by Friday**.
- **The 15-hour supervised test runs in Qingming mock week**, unless the school sets a dedicated window.
- **≈ end April 2028: coursework marks and samples go to Cambridge** — on the linear entry, C1, C2 and C3 all land at this single deadline. The June 2028 timetable publishes ≈ Oct 2027.
- Submit **two artists**: single-artist submissions score in the middle and low bands.
