# Liesbet van Zoonen

Named in the 2028 Scheme of Work as a representation theorist — **surname only, no idea attributed**.

## What Cambridge attributes to her

> "candidates need to learn the concept of Representation - best done with reference to theories of representation where there is a variety of student-friendly materials available - such as **Hall, Van Zoonen, hooks, Gilroy, Alvarado**." (2028 SoW, C2 Section A camera row)

That single clause is the complete attribution — no first name, no work, no concept. The identification as Liesbet van Zoonen (feminist media theory) is contextual: the clause is about representation of "class, age, gender, ethnicity," and van Zoonen is the standard media-studies reference for the gender strand.

## Status change

Until the 2028 SoW was ingested (2026-07-20), van Zoonen was filed as a **BNDS addition** — taught in [[classes/media-studies/lesson-plans/9607-s1-lesson-09-the-gaze|L09]] with no Cambridge naming anywhere. She is now Cambridge-suggested, which retroactively endorses the L09 design. The *idea* taught in L09 — gender as performance, worked on advertisements — remains a centre attribution; Cambridge names her without saying what to use her for.

## BNDS usage

L09 D1–2: gender as performance, worked on two ads, before Mulvey's three looks are stated precisely. See the lesson plan for the decade-pair stress-testing design.
