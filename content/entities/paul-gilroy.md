# Paul Gilroy

Paul Gilroy is a representation theorist named in the Cambridge-suggested roster for representation theory.

## What Cambridge attributes to him

> "candidates need to learn the concept of Representation - best done with reference to theories of representation where there is a variety of student-friendly materials available - such as **Hall, Van Zoonen, hooks, Gilroy, Alvarado**."

This concerns the representation of class, age, gender and ethnicity in Section A analysis.

## Status in the course

Gilroy is not currently taught at BNDS. The S1 representation block ([[classes/media-studies/unit-plans/9607-s1-unit-4-representation|U4]]) uses Hall, van Zoonen, Mulvey and hooks.
