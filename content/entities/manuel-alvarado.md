# Manuel Alvarado

Named in the 2028 Scheme of Work as a representation theorist — **surname only, no idea attributed**. New to the Cambridge-suggested roster; the from-2021 Scheme of Work never mentioned him.

## What Cambridge attributes to him

> "candidates need to learn the concept of Representation - best done with reference to theories of representation where there is a variety of student-friendly materials available - such as **Hall, Van Zoonen, hooks, Gilroy, Alvarado**." (2028 SoW, C2 Section A camera row)

The complete attribution. The identification as Manuel Alvarado is contextual: the clause concerns representation of "class, age, gender, ethnicity," and Alvarado is the standard A Level media-studies reference for racial-stereotype categories, usually taught alongside Hall and Gilroy.

## Status in the course

**Not currently taught at BNDS.** No BNDS plan uses him; like [[entities/paul-gilroy]], he is a candidate for deepening the ethnicity strand of [[concepts/representation]] in the G12 year rather than a gap in the current S1 plan.
